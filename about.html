FAQ

Do I need an account to try ALYSSIUN?

No. You can enter LITE worlds in your browser without an account. Some HD/VR features may require sign-in later.

What engines are supported?

We don’t disclose our web stack. For HD, ALYSSIUN uses Unreal Engine.

How do creators earn 80%?

Publish assets, apps, worlds, or mods to the marketplace. Sales revenue is split 80/20 in favor of creators.

Is VR supported?

Yes — both LITE (Web) and HD (Windows) support VR entry paths.

Is ALYSSIUN free?

Yes. The universe is free to enter via LITE or HD. White-Label is an optional, paid service for branded products.
